What is Abhikarti Foundation?

Abhikrati Foundation is solely operated by college students, founded on 9th March, 2020. Initially it was started with 5 to 6 members only, but now we're having a happy family of almost 150 plus members. It mainly focuses on providing education to marginalized communities specially in slum area. Currently in COVID pandemic, Abhikrati is engaging in Blood Donation Camps and we're also trying to provide food and essential items to needy ones. All the members of our team celebrate almost every festival with slum people and try to make smile on their faces. Also in Abhikrati we organize weekly teaching drives for slum people. Overall, it was a great journey till now and it will be too.
