Blood Donation Camp

blood donation is increasing day by day. Each year millions of people need blood transfusions. Major diseases like dengue, sickle cell anaemia, certain types of cancers, surgeries, accidents, natural disasters etc have been the main cause for blood transfusions around the world. Many lives are lost due to unavailability of blood. Doctors need blood for transfusing it in the body of person after operation and similar situations when a lot of blood flows out of the person’s body.
